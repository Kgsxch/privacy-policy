Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 2024

Overview

This privacy policy applies to all iOS applications developed by our company. Our apps are designed with privacy as a fundamental principle. We believe your personal information belongs to you, which is why we develop apps that keep your data local and private.

Information Collection and Use

Our applications are designed to respect your privacy. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers. All data is stored locally on your device and remains under your control.

Local Data Storage:

Depending on the specific app, we may store the following types of data locally on your device:

  • User-created content (such as tasks, notes, journal entries)
  • App preferences and settings
  • Display preferences and content organization

Data Storage and Security

All data is stored locally on your device using Apple's secure storage systems. This data:

  • Remains exclusively on your device
  • Is only accessible through our apps
  • Is never transmitted to external servers
  • Is protected by your device's built-in security features

Data Control

You maintain complete control over your data. You can:

  • Add or delete content at any time within our apps
  • Remove all stored data by uninstalling the app
  • Manage your data using your device's built-in settings

Third-Party Services

Our apps:

  • Do not include any third-party analytics
  • Do not use tracking tools
  • Do not share data with third parties
  • Do not include advertisements

Children's Privacy

Our applications do not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. All data created within our apps remains locally on the device and under parental control.

Future Changes

If we make any changes to our privacy practices:

  • We will update this privacy policy
  • We will notify users within the affected apps
  • We will maintain our commitment to user privacy

Your Consent

By using any of our applications, you consent to this privacy policy.
